💡 Pro Tips for Finding Business Opportunities

🎯Target Growth Signals

Look for companies announcing funding, expansion, or new product launches - they often need external services to support growth.

⏰Timing Matters

Recent announcements (2-6 months old) are ideal - companies have made decisions but may not have implemented solutions yet.

🔍Look for Pain Points

Companies mentioning challenges, struggles, or "need help" are actively seeking solutions and more likely to engage.

🌐Multiple Platforms

Use different search engines and try LinkedIn, Twitter, and company blogs for varied perspectives and opportunities.
